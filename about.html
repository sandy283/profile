About

Researcher across language, vision & control

A bit about my background, how I work, and the foundations I've built across LLMs, computer vision and reinforcement learning.

I'm a final-year dual-degree student at IIT Kharagpur, pursuing a B.Tech (Hons.) in Electronics & Electrical Communication Engineering and an M.Tech in Vision & Intelligent Systems, advised by Dr. Pawan Goyal.

My work spans large language models, computer vision and reinforcement learning. I'm currently a Research Intern at Ericsson working on continuous RL control via LLMs; I built policy-aware agents to secure AI at IBM Research, led post- and continual-training of industrial LLMs as AI & LLM Lead at Tecnod8.ai, and designed multi-agent data systems at Western Digital.

I care about models that are not just accurate but useful and trustworthy — from fine-tuning, distillation and pruning of LLMs, to GAN-based vision pipelines and agentic systems. I enjoy taking a research idea all the way to a working system that someone can actually use.

  • LLM post-training, distillation & pruning — distilled models surpassed GPT-4o-mini by 13%.
  • Two M.Tech theses and research papers currently in progress.
