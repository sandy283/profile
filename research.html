Research projects

Adversarial Multi-Scale Anomaly Detection

Dr. Ye Zhu · Deakin University

Multi-scale anomaly detection with a student–teacher network and feature pyramids, reaching 0.98+ AUC on MVTec-AD. A ResNeXt-50 backbone with adversarial training improved precision by 15%, with ROC > 0.98 across all categories.

Anomaly DetectionStudent–TeacherResNeXt-50

Drone-Image Acacia Detection with GANs

Dr. Daphne T. C. Lai · Univ. of Brunei

Automated acacia-tree detection from drone imagery using a conditional GAN with an attention-based U-Net. Multi-scale patch extraction and HSI features, with a residual-attention U-Net discriminator, achieved a best-in-class 95.73% mIoU.

Conditional GANAttention U-NetSegmentation

A policy automation & compliance engine using Policy-Aware ReAct agents, RBAC, and a Rego code generator with Agentic-RAG to validate and enforce LLM compliance. (PPO received)
